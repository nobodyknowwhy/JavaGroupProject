What is Rhine Lab?

    Rhine Lab is a leading technology company located on Terra,dedicated to developing cutting-edge technologies and making them available to the world. With its headquarters in Triemen, Rhine Lab has a strong presence in the global market, providing innovative solutions in various fields of science and engineering.

    One of Rhine Lab's most notable achievements is the invention of artificial neurotransmitters that can sense tiny vibrations in the environment. This breakthrough was made possible by the Originium Art Section, led by Dorothy Frank, and the Energy Section, led by Ferdinand Clooney. The neurotransmitters have a wide range of applications, from medical imaging to earthquake detection.

    Another notable product of Rhine Lab is the Starry Garden, a futuristic ecosystem created by the Ecological Section, led by Muelsyse. The Starry Garden is a self-contained environment that simulates the natural cycles of life, providing a sustainable source of food and oxygen for human settlements on other planets.
